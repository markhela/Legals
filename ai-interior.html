Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

AI Interior ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Photos and Camera Data: We request access to your device's camera and photo library to enable you to take or select photos of rooms for AI-powered interior design suggestions. These photos are processed locally on your device and through our secure AI service.
  • Generated Content: The interior design images generated through our service are stored locally on your device.
  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you use the app to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Generate AI-powered interior design suggestions
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Process purchases for Pro features

Data Storage

All photos and generated images are stored locally on your device. We do not permanently store your original photos on our servers. Images are only temporarily processed by our AI service to generate interior design suggestions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. The only sharing capabilities within the app are user-initiated through the iOS share sheet, allowing you to save or share your generated designs.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored within the app
  • Delete your data by removing the app
  • Control photo and camera permissions through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
